What is Swishlist?

I know I'm not the only one who spends thirty minutes shopping online only tp find that when I return an hour later, everything has disappeared from my cart. It's one of the worst feelings, but with Swishlist you don't have to experience it anymore. Swishlist is an online wishlist that saves everything you want for later. Simply save the links to different pieces in their designated folders and shop on your own time.

So how do I Swishlist?

  1. Find something you like on one of the provided websites, or press the plus at the top of the screen to add a folder for other websites

  2. Drag and drop the link to the specific piece of clothing into the pink box that says "Drag links here!"

  3. Save all of your favorite clothes so that you can buy them later instead of starting from scratch--happy shopping!
